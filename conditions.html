Conditions We Treat â€” Healing Begins with Understanding

We provide expert diagnosis and care for a wide range of psychiatric conditions. Our goal is to not only treat the symptoms but also address the underlying causes to ensure lasting recovery.

We treat various anxiety disorders including:

  • Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)
  • Panic Disorder
  • Social Anxiety Disorder
  • Specific Phobias
  • Separation Anxiety Disorder

Our approach combines therapy, medication when appropriate, and lifestyle modifications to help you manage anxiety effectively.

Comprehensive treatment for mood disorders including:

  • Major Depressive Disorder
  • Bipolar Disorder (Type I & II)
  • Persistent Depressive Disorder
  • Seasonal Affective Disorder
  • Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder

We provide personalized treatment plans that may include medication management, psychotherapy, and lifestyle interventions.

Specialized treatment for:

  • Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
  • Body Dysmorphic Disorder
  • Hoarding Disorder
  • Trichotillomania (Hair-Pulling Disorder)
  • Excoriation Disorder (Skin-Picking)

We use evidence-based treatments including Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP) and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy.

Compassionate care for trauma-related conditions:

  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Acute Stress Disorder
  • Adjustment Disorders
  • Reactive Attachment Disorder

Our trauma-informed approach focuses on safety, trust, and evidence-based treatments like EMDR and Trauma-Focused CBT.

Specialized assessment and treatment for:

  • Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
  • Autism Spectrum Disorders
  • Learning Disorders
  • Intellectual Disabilities
  • Communication Disorders

We provide comprehensive evaluations and develop individualized treatment plans for children, adolescents, and adults.

Expert care for psychotic disorders:

  • Schizophrenia
  • Schizoaffective Disorder
  • Brief Psychotic Disorder
  • Delusional Disorder

We provide comprehensive treatment including medication management, psychotherapy, and family support.

Specialized treatment for personality disorders:

  • Borderline Personality Disorder
  • Narcissistic Personality Disorder
  • Antisocial Personality Disorder
  • Avoidant Personality Disorder
  • Obsessive-Compulsive Personality Disorder

We use evidence-based approaches like Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) and Schema Therapy.

Comprehensive addiction treatment for:

  • Alcohol Use Disorder
  • Drug Use Disorders
  • Gambling Disorder
  • Internet Gaming Disorder
  • Dual Diagnosis (Mental Health + Substance Use)

Our approach includes medical detoxification, medication-assisted treatment, and ongoing support.

Treatment for sleep disorders related to mental health:

  • Insomnia Disorder
  • Sleep-Wake Disorders
  • Nightmare Disorder
  • Sleep-related breathing disorders

We address both the sleep issues and underlying mental health conditions for comprehensive care.

Specialized care for cognitive decline:

  • Alzheimer's Disease
  • Vascular Dementia
  • Lewy Body Dementia
  • Frontotemporal Dementia
  • Mild Cognitive Impairment

We provide comprehensive assessment, treatment, and family support for cognitive disorders.
