ABOUT WELLMORE

About Wellmore Mental Health Services

We are a dedicated team of mental health professionals—Psychiatrist, Psychiatric Social Worker, and Clinical Psychologist—trained at the prestigious National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS), Bangalore. United by a shared vision and deep commitment to mental wellness, we have come together to offer accessible, compassionate, and evidence-based mental health services through a secure online platform.

With years of clinical experience and academic training from one of India’s premier mental health institutions, our team understands the complex emotional, psychological, and social challenges that individuals, families, and communities face in today’s world. We believe that timely and personalized support can transform lives, and we aim to bridge the gap between experts and those in need of mental health care.

What Sets Us Apart

A holistic approach with specialists working collaboratively to offer mental health care.

Grounded in the highest standards of clinical ethics and evidence-based practice.

Respect for your values, privacy, and pace.

Our services are designed to be inclusive, acknowledging and integrating the cultural values and life contexts of each individual.

With flexible fee structures based on socioeconomic background, we ensure mental health support is not a luxury but a right.
